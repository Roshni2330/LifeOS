# 🌌 LifeOS AI

> **"Meet your future before you live it."**

LifeOS AI is an AI-powered decision simulator that helps users visualize multiple possible futures before making one of the biggest decisions of their life.

Instead of simply giving advice, LifeOS creates a **Digital Twin**, simulates different future timelines, compares outcomes, explains trade-offs, and even lets users have a conversation with their future self.

Built for the **OpenAI × NamasteDev Hackathon 2026**.

---

# 🚀 The Problem

Every day people make decisions that completely change their future.

- Should I take the job?
- Should I prepare for GATE?
- Should I pursue higher studies?
- Should I build a startup?
- Should I move abroad?

Traditional AI assistants only answer questions.

They **don't simulate consequences.**

---

# 💡 Our Solution

LifeOS AI creates a **Digital Twin** of the user.

Using goals, priorities, personality and life preferences, it generates multiple future universes showing:

- Career Growth
- Financial Stability
- Learning
- Stress
- Happiness
- Regret
- Relationships
- Health

Users don't just receive recommendations.

They **experience their future** before making the decision.

---

# ✨ Core Features

## 🧬 Digital Twin

Creates an AI representation of the user.

---

## 🌍 Multiverse Simulation

Generates three different future timelines.

Example:

Universe A → Job

Universe B → Higher Studies

Universe C → Startup

---

## 📈 Life Score

Every future receives explainable scores for:

- Career
- Money
- Learning
- Health
- Relationships
- Happiness
- Regret
- Stress

---

## ⚔️ Decision Battle

Compare any two futures side by side.

LifeOS explains:

- Which future wins
- Why it wins
- Tradeoffs
- Confidence Score

---

## 🦋 Butterfly Effect

Change one small habit like:

- Study hours
- Exercise
- Networking
- Projects

Watch the future change instantly.

---

## 💬 Future Self Chat

Talk directly with your future self.

Example:

> "Was choosing GATE worth it?"

Your future self answers based on the simulated timeline.

---

## 📅 Future Timeline

Travel year-by-year through your simulated life.

See:

- Major milestones
- Promotions
- Salary growth
- Life events
- Risks
- Opportunities
